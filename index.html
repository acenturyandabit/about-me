Passion: Primary Sector Renewability

I believe the human race is capable of progressing into the future sustainably without compromising our standard of living. This could only be achieved by developing and implementing renewable resource streams. Only if we can do this in a scalable manner will we be able to pursue longer term goals such as increased world stability, a world free from disease, and space exploration.

There are many ways that I see myself going about this, whether it's working with AI and NLP to analyse data and identify opportunities for resource recycling, or engineering scalability and reliability into the latest scientific advances.

Passion: Strategy

Our consciousness enables us to plan far into the future and I believe that we should utilise this in everything that we do. I strongly appreciate responsive, self-aware organisations which have a vision and a well-thought-out means of achieving it.

I feel like as a society we arm ourselves with tools to deal with the short term rather than the long term. I want to change this by helping develop new ways of conceptualising and planning ideas to facilitate strategic thinking.

Passion: Robotics and automation

As humans one of our defining features is our culture, founded upon self-expression and art. If we constrain people into repetitive tasks that really could be done by a robot, we are putting a cap on our abilities as a species. This is why I'm passionate about robotics - not only as a means to be more efficient, but also as a pathway for us as a species to become better than we are today.

I would love to take part in a project which aims to bring down the cost of robotics whilst increasing its capabilities so that robotics becomes a part of our everyday life which we can depend on to get basic things done without much intervention.
